# Sweep Unleashed

## A Modern Take on Classic Minesweeper with Explosive Abilities and Strategic Depth!

Sweep Unleashed reimagines the timeless Minesweeper experience with a futuristic, arcade-inspired aesthetic and dynamic abilities. Dive into a grid-based battlefield where every click can uncover fortune or trigger a devastating mine. Strategize your moves, deploy powerful abilities, and climb the global leaderboard to become the ultimate mine-sweeping champion!

## Features

*   **Classic Minesweeper Gameplay**: Uncover cells, flag mines, and clear the board with familiar yet enhanced mechanics.
*   **Dynamic Abilities**: Utilize powerful abilities like "Scan" to find safe cells, "Detonate" to clear areas, "Shield" for protection, "Judgement Cut End" for a cinematic mine blast, and "Jane Juliet" to clear large sections of the field. Each ability has a unique cost and impact on gameplay.
*   **Customizable Loadouts**: Choose your preferred field size (Small, Medium, Large) and an ability to take into battle.
*   **Player Progression**: Earn coins, track your wins, games played, and win streaks.
*   **Stylish Skins**: Personalize your game with various cosmetic skins like Neon, Cyberpunk, City, and Sakura.
*   **Daily Quests**: Complete daily challenges to earn extra coins and rewards.
*   **Leaderboard**: Compete with other players globally and prove your mine-sweeping prowess.
*   **Sound Effects**: Immersive sound design enhances every click, reveal, and ability activation.

## How to Play

1.  **Select Loadout**: From the main menu, choose your desired field size and a unique ability.
2.  **Uncover Cells**: Click on cells to reveal them.
    *   If a number is revealed, it indicates how many mines are adjacent to that cell.
    *   If an empty cell is revealed, it will automatically clear all adjacent empty cells.
3.  **Flag Mines**: Right-click (or long-press on mobile) on a cell to flag it as a suspected mine.
4.  **Use Abilities**: Accumulate charges by revealing cells. Once you have enough charges, activate your chosen ability to gain a strategic advantage.
5.  **Win the Game**: Successfully reveal all non-mine cells to win!
6.  **Avoid Mines**: If you click on a mine without a shield, it's game over!
